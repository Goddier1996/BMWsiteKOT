Let"s See The Sport"s Cars By BMW M:

BMW M5

The BMW M5 is a high performance variant of the BMW 5 Series marketed under the BMW M sub-brand. It is considered an iconic vehicle in the sports sedan category.

BMW M3

The BMW M3 is a high-performance version of the BMW 3 Series, developed by BMW's in-house motorsport division, BMW M GmbH. ... Since 2015 the M3 has been solely produced in the sedan.

BMW M2

It runs on new 405-horsepower, turbocharged 3.0-liter six-cylinder engine that works with standard six-speed manual transmission or an optional seven-speed dual-clutch automatic.

BMW M8

The M8 will be powered by a twin-turbocharged 4.4-liter V-8, which also motivates the BMW M5. The standard M8 engine makes 600 horsepower and 553 lb-ft of torque.

The History With BMW:

The official founding date of the German motor vehicle manufacturer BMW is 7 March 1916, when an aircraft engine manufacturer called Bayerische Flugzeugwerke AG was formed. This company was renamed to Bayerische Motoren Werke in 1922.
